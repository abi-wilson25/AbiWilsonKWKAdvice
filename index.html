Helpful Advice for Camp!

General advice and tips on how to apply to camp, have fun at camp, and deal with post camp depression.

  • How to Apply

    Applicants need to write a short essay, and also film a small video to apply to camp. Check out the Kode With Klossy website to get the full instructions on how to apply.

  • What to Bring to Camp

    While most supplies, and snacks are already provided, a water bottle may help you stay hydrated during camp, and a postive attitude may help you make friends and get the most from the experience.

  • What to do Before Camp

    Get a good night's rest, because you are about to have tons of fun at camp. Also have a good healthy breakfast so that you'll have the energy to make it through the day.

  • What to do at Camp

    Have an open mind, be open experiences, and try to make friends with the like-minded girls who are just as excited to start camp as you are. Kode with Klossy is a super fun, and helpful experience.

  • What to do after Camp

    Share your experience on social media using #kodewithlossy, to help you find girls who attended other camps, and inspire more to follow in your footsteps. While the end of camp can be sad, focus on the knowledge you gained at camp, and try using it to pursue your own dreams! Coding can be used in any field, and help with almost any project. So even if camp is over, or maybe you never attended at all, the knowleddge of coding can still help you in your everyday life.
